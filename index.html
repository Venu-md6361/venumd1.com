One’s learning alone is one’s indestructible and outstanding wealth, nothing else possess this special value. (Thirukkural-kural 400). The wealth of learning gives a rare joy that nothing else can really give to the possessor.To gift this everlasting wealth to the youth of the malur taluk and fulfill their aspiration for higher education, Government of Karnataka sanctioned a First Grade College with Arts and Commerce courses vide Government Order Number ED 99 Plan-University 88 Bangalore, Dated 07-09-1988. Thus, the government first grade college malur has seen the light of the day in 1988. The college is now affiliated to Bangalore University, covered undersection 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act and reaccredited ‘B’ grade in cycle-2 by NAAC in the year 2013.The college which was started in a small building donated by generous philanthropists, with only two courses and hundred and odd students comprising both boys and girls in the beginning have increased significantly to 2037 during the academic year 2016-17 and the number of final year students it churned out during the last five years from 2012-13 to 2016-17 have been 2974 showing an average of 595 students per year. Presently the college functioning in two different college buildings with good infrastructure facilities; one at main heart of the city and another new one near malur railway station. In both, the college now offers three undergraduate programmes and one postgraduate programmes in economics taught by well qualified teachers, supported by experienced administrative staff and rightly guided by the College Development Council. The faculty credentials are impeccable and students academic performances have been remarkable which all reflects ina student of our college bagging 8 gold medals in kannada subject in 2013-14, an other student getting a gold medal in commerce subject in the year 2014-15 and 14 of scouts guides students receiving 14 Rashtra and Rajya Puraskar Awards and guidesin 2015-16 and 2016-17. To add this achievement list, the college can also boast of having successfully organized number of training programmes, workshops, two state and three national level conferences on national and global importance for the academic benefit of all.

Apart from the commitment to the quest for knowledge, students in our college are provided with facilities such as sports, Gym, Yoga, NCC, National Social Service (NSS), Scouts and Guides, recreation, cultural and so on. Several enrichment programmes are also offered through Edusat, skill development, virtual class, orientation programmes, career guidance, counselling, inter class, inter-collegiate events, college day and so on. The college has undertaken all these tasks with atmost zeal for the all-round development of the students. During its 30 years of its meaningful existence the college contributed significantly towards enrichment of knowledge in various fields and meeting the higher educational needs of the society. Its Alumni are spread across the country, holding elite and coveted positions as teachers, police officers, lawyers, politicians, entrepreneurs, educationists, writers, artists and other responsible status.
